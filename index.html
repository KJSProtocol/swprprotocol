Why dust matters

Billions in lost value across Web3.

Every wallet. Every chain. Every user. Over time, dust accumulates: leftover decimals, gas fragments, abandoned airdrops, conversion remainders. These tiny fragments are too small to trade or move efficiently, but at scale they represent billions of dollars of trapped value across the ecosystem.

Trapped value, everywhere.

Users see cluttered balances and unusable fragments. Networks see friction and waste. There is no unified, protocol-level way to reclaim dust as clean, usable value.

  • Conversion leftovers from swaps and bridges.
  • Stranded gas tokens on chains a user no longer touches.
  • Airdrop remnants and forgotten micro-balances.
Cluttered UX. Fragmented value.

The result is the same: balances that look noisy but act empty. Most users simply write dust off as “lost.” SWPR was built on the belief that no honest value should be abandoned by design.

The SWPR solution

A universal dust recovery engine.

SWPR Protocol introduces a clean, fair system to gather, convert, and consolidate dust across supported chains. Instead of sitting as noise and waste, dust becomes a liquid, redeemable asset again.

Smart detection & aggregation.

Wallets and nodes can integrate a SWPR-aware scan to identify fragments below a chosen threshold across supported networks. These pieces are bundled into sweep events instead of being left behind forever.

  • Configurable dust thresholds per chain and asset.
  • Non-custodial design principles at the core.
  • Seamless integration paths for wallets, DEXs, and protocols.
Fair conversion. Transparent economics.

The protocol is designed so users can see how fragments are converted, how SWPR is minted or allocated, and how fees and rewards are distributed. Oversight and parameters can be governed by a DAO over time.

  • Clear, auditable rules for dust-to-SWPR conversion.
  • Transparent fees, rewards, and burn / sink mechanics.
  • Designed for builders, not black-box extraction.
How it works

A simple, protocol-driven 3-step loop.

Under the hood, SWPR can support sophisticated routing, routing fees, and chain-specific logic. But for the end user and the builder, the model stays simple:

Step 1
Detect

Wallets or SWPR-integrated tools scan balances to identify dust — fragments too small to use, trade, or bridge efficiently. No action is taken without explicit permission.

Step 2
Sweep

Approved dust is aggregated and routed into the SWPR recovery engine, where a protocol-defined conversion path turns scattered value into unified SWPR units.

Step 3
Redeem

Users receive consolidated, spendable value instead of noisy, abandoned fragments. What was previously written off as “lost” can be stored, moved, or deployed again.

SWPR token

Protocol fuel with real utility.

The SWPR token is not an afterthought. It is wired into how dust is swept, how rewards are distributed, and how parameters evolve over time.

As the dust recovery engine powers more integrations, SWPR can sit at the center of a growing value loop: the more dust reclaimed, the more meaningful the role of the token.

At its core, SWPR is about returning stranded value to the people who own it — transparently, fairly, and at scale. Any token model aligned with that principle should be easy to explain and easier to audit.

SWPR was born from a simple observation: people quietly accept loss when systems are designed to leave small pieces behind. Dust is treated as inevitable waste instead of something worth recovering.


This protocol is the opposite of that thinking. It is for everyday users, long-term builders, and anyone who believes that honest value shouldn’t be abandoned by default. The smallest pieces still matter.

Design principles.
  • Fair by design: Users should understand how value is swept, converted, and returned.
  • Builder-friendly: Easy integration paths for wallets, DEXs, and protocols.
  • Transparent economics: No black-box extraction. Clear sinks, sources, and rewards.
  • Long-term focus: Dust recovery as an enduring, structural layer in Web3.
